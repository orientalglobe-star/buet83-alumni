Our Reunion Legacy

2025
Grand Reunion — Radisson Blu, Dhaka. 150+ alumni gathered. First reunion with dedicated children's program.
2023
40th Anniversary Reunion — BUET Campus. Historic milestone — returned to where it all began. 180 alumni attended from 18 countries.
2020
Virtual Reunion — COVID-19 Edition. First ever virtual reunion via Zoom. 220 alumni connected from across the world.
2018
35th Anniversary Reunion — Pan Pacific, Dhaka. Grand celebration with cultural program and batch documentary screening.
2013
30th Anniversary Reunion — BUET Auditorium. Landmark event that launched the BUET83 Scholarship Fund.
2003
20th Anniversary Reunion — Dhaka Sheraton. The first large-scale reunion with international alumni participation.
1988
First Reunion — BUET Campus. The very first gathering of the batch — 5 years after graduation. 45 alumni attended.
