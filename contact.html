Common Questions

FAQ

How do I add myself to the Members Directory? ▼
Send your details (name, department, current city, country, and role) to committee@buet83alumni.org and we'll add you within 48 hours.
How do I submit a news story or achievement? ▼
Use the contact form above or email us directly. Include a brief write-up and any photos. We'll feature it on the News page within a few days.
How do I RSVP for an event? ▼
Visit the Events page and click the RSVP button for the relevant event. You can also contact your chapter representative directly.
How do I contribute to the Scholarship Fund? ▼
Email scholarship@buet83alumni.org for bank details. All contributions are acknowledged and published in the newsletter with your permission.
How do I share old photos from our BUET days? ▼
Email your photos to committee@buet83alumni.org or use the contact form. Please include the year and any context so we can caption them correctly.
